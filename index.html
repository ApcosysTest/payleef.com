Automated Tax and Allowance calculation.

Allowances in cost estimates, tax, payroll, incentives, and bonuses can already be calculated by Payleef. Payleef can also automatically recalculate specific allowances when it detects a change in an employee record or data table using allowance automation.

  • Enter CTC breakup according to yours comapny policy.
  • Payleef automatically calculates taxes and deductions.
  • Then, it also calculates allowances and incentives.
  • Finally it generates payslip according to the calculated values.

Employee Details Registration and Modification

Employee setup is completed initially where the admin will add his or her information, including the CTC and allowances, and the automated feature will do the necessary calculations and generate the salary report and payslip accordingly. This reduces the workload of the Human resources department, which in turn minimises the paperwork and increases the effectiveness of the payroll system.

  • Enter employee details mannually and provide specific details like CTC.
  • Upload Employee details in bulk along with all the necessary calculation criterias.
  • Modify employee details and calculation criterias along with CTC.

Secured System

Get all new security features with Payleef and enhance the data management. Payleef makes data i.e employee records along with the company details more organized and easily accessible. Payleef provides with cent percent accuracy along with ensuring data is safe and reliable and there is no data breach.
